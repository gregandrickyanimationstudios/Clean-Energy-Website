Nuclear power

A bad wrap

Nuclear power isn't that bad as you might think it is, Let us explain.

Two types

There are 2 types of Nuclear Power. It can be Fission or Fusion. Fission is splitting atoms, and Fusion is putting them back together. Currently we cannot do fusion, but we *can* de Fission! Splitting the atom can be very dangerous, but all of the safety persuasions are necessary.

How it works

What most Nuclear power plants use is atoms of Uranium 235. When the atoms split it releases tremendous energy. This is enough to power a 100 watt light bulb for over 690 years. Also Small Nuclear Reactor is more safe. (see Real Talk) Also If you see a Nuclear Power Plant. Just know that steam is coming from the cooling tower, not carbon dioxide.
